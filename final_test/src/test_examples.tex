$\mu_A(x) = \{\frac{0.2}{1}, \frac{0.7}{2}, \frac{0.9}{3}, \frac{1.0}{4}, \frac{0.1}{5}\}$ \\ [0.3em]
$\mu_B(y) = \{\frac{1.0}{10}, \frac{0.8}{20}, \frac{0.4}{30}, \frac{0.2}{40}, \frac{0.1}{50}\}$
\\[0.7em]
$\mu_N(z) = \{\frac{0.2}{-1}, \frac{0.5}{0}, \frac{1.0}{1}\}$ \\ [0.3em]
$\mu_M(z) = \{\frac{0.7}{-1}, \frac{0.9}{0}, \frac{0.2}{1}\}$ \\ [0.3em]
$\mu_K(z) = \{\frac{0.2}{-1}, \frac{0.6}{0}, \frac{0.9}{1}\}$ \\

1. if $y$ is $\neg B$ then $z$ is $N$ \\
2. if $x$ is $A$ $\land$ $y$ is $B$ then $z$ is $M$ \\
3. if $x$ is $\neg A$ $\land$ $y$ is $B$ then $z$ is $\neg K$ \\

$x=2; y=30; z=?$
